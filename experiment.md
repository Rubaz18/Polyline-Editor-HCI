# Fitts’ Law Experiment

##  What is Fitts’ Law?

Fitts’ Law states that the time required to move to a target depends on:

* Distance to target
* Size of target

---

##  Implementation

The system:

1. Generates a random target
2. Records:

   * Distance from click
   * Time taken
3. Repeats for multiple trials

---

##  Metrics Collected

* Movement Time (seconds)
* Distance (pixels)

---

##  Output

Average time and distance are displayed on screen.

---

##  Purpose

To analyze user performance and validate Fitts’ Law in a simple UI environment.
